Final Year Engineering (BTech)

The final year (BTech) is the most crucial phase of engineering, where students prepare for placements, higher studies, or entrepreneurship. The emphasis is on project work, industrial collaboration, and skill refinement.

Key highlights of the final year:

  • Major Project / Capstone Project
  • Elective and Advanced Technical Courses
  • Internship or Industry-Oriented Training
  • Preparation for Placements and Competitive Exams (GATE, GRE, etc.)
  • Soft Skills and Professional Development
