Third Year Engineering (TY)

In the third year of engineering, students start diving into specialized subjects related to their branch. This year is crucial for technical skill development, industry exposure, and practical learning.

Key focus areas in the third year include:

  • Core subjects based on specialization (e.g., AI, IoT, Mechanical Design, VLSI, etc.)
  • Software and Hardware Implementation
  • Database Management and Web Technologies
  • Internships and Industrial Training
  • Mini Projects and Research Work
